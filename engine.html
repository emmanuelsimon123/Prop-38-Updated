    How to use this page

    This is an interactive statement–reason proof with a linked diagram.

    • Next / Back: step through the proof and watch highlights update.
    • Hover bold geometry (e.g., ABC, BC): highlights the matching diagram parts.
    • Progress bar: click to jump. Keyboard: focus bar then ←/→.
    • Focus: dims everything except the relevant objects.
    • Explore: drag handles to see the construction change.
    • Practice modes: choose statements/reasons to unlock steps.
    • Keyboard: ←/→ step, Space play/pause, R reset.

    Tip: For side-by-side (two columns), widen the window or zoom out. For stacked (one column), narrow the window or zoom in.
